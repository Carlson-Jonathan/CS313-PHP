About Us:

Rocket City Rebels (formerly Rad Recruits) is a co-ed junior roller derby team in north Alabama for skaters ages 8 to 18 who are dedicated to learning the sport. Coaches teach skaters to respect themselves, teammates, coaches, officials, fans and opponents. All of this knowledge is used to build healthy bodies, healthy minds, and healthy lifestyles on and off the track. Roller derby is a very physically and emotionally demanding sport. It is also a very rewarding and character building adventure.
